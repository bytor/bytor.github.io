Music Mail Order Stores

This is a list of music mail order stores on the web ONLY. Although some stores now have email addresses, this file is only for those with a web presence. If you know of a store that I don't have listed, please let me know.

Last Updated: May 8, 1997




A B CD's
CD singles and albums, both new and used. Also carry imports. Will take credit card orders through the web (or email). Located in Eastern Washington (maybe Spokane?). Phone: (509) 483-2381.

Backstage Distributions
Carry tons of collectibles, including autographed merchandise, rare posters, photos, cds, videos and clothing. They are a major supplier of rare items to the Hard Rock Cafe. Don't seem to have any Rush, but you never know... Page is very graphic-intensive, turn off graphic loading if you have a slow connection! Located in Quebec, Canada. Phone: (800) 607-ROCK

CBL Records
Have lots of collectibles including cd singles, vinyl, posters and video bootlegs (they can also convert videos from NTSC to PAL format). Do not currently accept credit card orders, only money orders and checks. Located in San Francisco, California.

The CD Cellar
WARNING! Be VERY careful when purchasing items through CD Cellar. Several people have reported that they've never received items they've purchased and never got their money back!!!

CDNow
Carry import CDs, cd singles, gold discs, some vinyl, t-shirts & videos. Have heard they do carry bootlegs, but none are listed on the web site. Will accept credit card orders through the web. Charge tax to residents in Connecticut, Pennsylvania, California, and some APO/FPO addresses. Located in Penllyn, Pennsylvania. Phone: (215) 654-8076 Fax: (215) 283-4965

CDworld
Carry CDs, videos, CD-ROMs, and gold discs. Will accept credit card orders through the web.

Cellophane Square
Collectible vinyl and cds, including promo items, rare releases and box sets. Doesn't have much listed in catalog, but have much more not shown. Will take want/trade lists. Located in Seattle, Washington (has two other stores close to Seattle) Phone: (206) 634-2280.

Compact Disc Connection
Have CDs, CD singles, use a secure server to process transactions via the web.

The Concert Artifacts of Legends
Carries concert posters and used backstage passes, has a separate Rush section with pictures. Will accept checks, money orders, and credit cards (you can purchase them through the internet by sending email with your credit card, although I wouldn't recommend it) Located in Portland, Oregon. Fax: (503) 256-4563

CVC Collectables
Has a separate Rush section on their catalog. Can get on a mailing list for all the latest updates. Carries all sorts of merchandise, but concentrates on vinyl. Located in Celina, Ohio. Phone: (419) 586-6636 Fax: (419) 586-6763

Dr. Wax
Used and collectible CD's and vinyl. Does not have their full catalog on-line, but instead has "featured" items. Quite a selection (including a few boots), good prices. The vinyl and collectibles run on an auction, the cd's are first-come. Located in Chicago, Illinois (has 4 stores in the area) Phone: (800) 435-9815. Open 11:30am - 9pm CDT.

EQS Music
Lots of collectibles, vinyl, posters, cd singles and radio shows. Catalog is listed by artist (and they always have some Rush). Located in Auburn, New York. Phone: (315) 252-1999. Mon-Wed-Fri 12pm-6pm, Tue-Thu 6pm-9pm.

(meg) - I've dealt with them for years. Very reliable, and very helpful on the phone.

Forever Vinyl
Carries vinyl exclusively. Catalog not on-line, but is a downloadable zip file. Picture discs, rare vinyl (including bootlegs), regular albums and samplers. Located in Lakehurst, New Jersey. Phone: (908) 505-3646

Gash Record Rescue
Doesn't have much up yet, but say they are cataloging thousands of items and putting a database up on the web. Apparently will deal with collectibles. Located in the UK.

Hot Platters
Carry all sorts of collectibles, including books, magazines, tapes, videos, posters, sheet music, photos, buttons, and CD's (currently only have cds and vinyl online). Items available through bids only. Located in Thousand Oaks, California. Fax: (805) 492-3682

i? music/media
Commercially available cds (albums and gold discs), cassettes, laser discs and VHS tapes. Appear to be able to take credit card orders through the web, but the link to ordering information (on another server) isn't working.

Intergalactic Garage Compact Discs
Have lots of cds, singles, bootlegs, vinyl, posters, books, magazines and other collectibles. Doesn't have too many boots listed, and posters do not have descriptions (although they claim they will be putting up pictures of many posters in the next few weeks). Will accept credit cards. Located in Shepherdstown, West Virginia. Phone: (304) 876-6818 Fax: (304) 876-9414. Open Tuesday - Sunday.

(meg) - when I emailed them about several posters, the person who responded was less than helpful. Would not describe the posters, nor offered to look at them for me. Mabye it's just the person answering email.

Lakeshore Record Exchange
Carry CDs, vinyl and videos, specializing in alternative music. Also have rare collectibles. Will accept credit cards. Located in Rochester, New York. Phone: (716) 244-8476 Fax: (716) 244-8421. Open 7 days a week.

Magic Com
Seem to carry bootleg CDs exclusively. Large selection, decent prices; shipping can be a bit much. Do NOT ship jewel cases with cds outside of Germany. Will accept credit cards or international money orders. Located in Gapenach, Germany. Phone: 011-49-2654-987010 Fax: 011-49-2654-987012

Music Boulevard
Commercially available cds and tapes. Consider it an on-line Tower Records. Good prices, not sure how much they charge for shipping. Loooks like you need an account with them to order anything, probably take credit cards through the web. Located in Pennsylvania. Phone: (800) 216-6000.

Music By Email
Imported cds (commercial and rare), mainly from Europe. Will take credit card orders through the web (sends via email), can also fax or call orders in. On-line order form automatically calculates shipping and tax. Phone: (800) 233-3000 or (407) 286-8502.

Music Connection
Commercial albums and gold discs, both cd and tape. Currently cannot order on-line, but will accept credit cards. Located in Palo Alto, California. Phone: (415) 329-9393.

Noteworthy Music
Import and domestic CDs (including gold discs). Not much selection online. Phone: (617) 441-5050

PastelBlue
Carry UK and US cds, cd singles, videos (PAL only). Will accept credit cards or international money orders, credit card orders are charged an additional 5%. Orders only accepted through email. Located in England.

Picture Palace
Carry laserdiscs and videos. Have movies in addition to music. Import laser discs on the expensive side. Will accept credit card orders through email. Located in Kearny, New Jersey. Fax: (201) 955-9109

POP!culture Collectibles
Carry assorted collectibles, posters, shirts, etc. Do not have a catalog on the web to browse, but must request it through email. Located in Beltsville, Maryland. Phone: (301) 931-0137

Rockworld
Lots of collectibles, posters, rare items. Most items have color pictures of them online. Will accept credit cards. Phone: (800) ROCK-112

RPM Records
Carry independent and import cds, vinyl, laser discs and videos. Also has a large selection of individual music videos to trade. Can also do special orders. Will accept credit cards. Located in Mesquite, Texas. Phone: (214) 681-8441. Open 7 days a week (also have a walk-in store).

Siren Disc
Carry CD and vinyl only. Have albums, singles and imports (NO bootlegs). Will accept credit cards, checks, and money orders. Located in Los Angeles, California (CA residents must add 8.25% tax to their order). Phone: (213) 935-0578. Open Monday - Friday.

TimeWarp Record Store
Carry new and used vinyl, also run auctions. Can submit want list. Will accept credit cards through the web. Located in Petaluma, California. Fax: (707) 766-9757

Video Online Express
Carry videos & laser discs. Good prices. Will accept credit card orders through the web. Located in San Jose, California. Phone: (408) 364-2250 Fax: (408) 364-2251
