I modified the "Rivendell" transcription originally posted by Kevin
McFadden.  What I actually did was put all of his sections in
order according to the recording.  I'm also including the accents 
as far as I can tell them.  Rhythm progessions are left to
the reader; we can't do all the work for you!
P.S.: Thanks to Kevin McFadden for teaching me how to play this
excellent acoustic song!
