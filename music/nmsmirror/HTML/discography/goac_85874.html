4.29 Red Tide 		DESCRIPTION
This cd single is one of the few released in a slim jewel case. It only has a front insert: green with a picture of the flying wishbones. The disc is also green with white print. Although this disc was manufactured in Germany, the packaging also lists France. It's unclear if two separate pressings were made for the two countries, or even if both countries sold the single.
