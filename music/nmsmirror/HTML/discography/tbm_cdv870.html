1985


TRACK LISTING
5.36 The Big Money
5.25 Red Sector A (live)
6.09 Marathon
4.52 The Big Money (video) 		DESCRIPTION
Although not a "true" cd single, it does contain audio tracks so is included here. This cd has the unique distinction of being Rush's only CDV, containing not only audio tracks but the video for "The Big Money." The US version is in NTSC format, while the UK version is in PAL format. The live track is from the "Grace Under Pressure" video soundtrack, never officially released on cd. This cd is becoming increasingly rare, as some of them have succumbed to laser rot (a large problem with the earliest laserdiscs).
