4.51 Double Agent 		DESCRIPTION
This is a promo cd single. It has a picture on of salt & pepper shakers on the front insert (from the "Counterparts" album), and the "star" on the back insert. The disc is black with silver writing. This cd single is similar in appearance to the other three singles released for "Counterparts."
