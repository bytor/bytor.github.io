--------|    above there are numbers with no space between them. I did this

in this transcription would be an 8th note then.

                                                         two 16th notes.
 
Notice that he doesn't play the double notes each time this section repeats.
One final note, the vertical bars in the transcription represent dividers for
sections of the song that seemed logical to me. They're not meant to be
measures.  Also any vertical line with two dots next to it means to repeat the
section. Again, listen to the recording to find out how many times to repeat.

Explanation on used symbols:

|  - A pair of these indicate a part of music, not the usuals bars.
:  - Indicates that a part is to be repeated. This may be ommited, you should 
     hear it too.
_  - Indicates a hammer-on or pull-off (You should know which is appropriate)
>  - Indicates a bend-up or down or a slide. With a bend, the note to bend to
     is between '(' and ')'. Use the whammy for a downbend. With a slide, the
     follow-up note is the one to be slid to.
!  - Indicates vibrato.
^  - Indicates harmonics. Is printed below the note.
