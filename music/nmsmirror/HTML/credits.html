The National Midnight Star Mirror pays tribute to the creators of the NMS. Every effort is being made to maintain the integrity of the original site, while at the same time upgrading  the html to take advantage of new browser technology. The NMS was more than just a web site is was Rush exemplified on the internet. When and if the NMS resumes operation this Mirror will be closed, but until then it will remain a living tribute to the worlds favorite Rush site, "The National Midnight Star"
