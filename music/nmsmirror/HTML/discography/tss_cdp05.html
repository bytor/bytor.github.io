Time Stand Still
Mercury CDP 05 (USA)
October 1987


TRACK LISTING
5.07 Time Stand Still
4.42 Time Stand Still (edit)
 DESCRIPTION
This is a promo cd single, and was Mercury's only released single for Rush. The front insert is the cover of "Hold Your Fire", the back insert is red with black print, and the disc has simple black print. The "edit" version is missing some of the instrumental part (most notably the beginning), but no lyrics were cut.
