A Note to the Bassist
______________________________________________________________________________
This is a really cool and easy to play song. Listen to the music for the 
exact rythms but I think everything is indicated correctly (at least as 
correctly as you can get with tablature). I transcribed everything from
Rush Complete Vol.1 exactly the way I play it, but play it in what ever 
position is easiest for you. The only notation symbol I used was a "_" 
which is just a legato (or "Hammer On" for you guys who haven't learned 
any theory).

	Have fun,
		-Andy
