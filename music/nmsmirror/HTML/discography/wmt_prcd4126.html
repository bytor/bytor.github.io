Where's My Thing?
Atlantic PRCD 4126-2 (USA)
November 1991


TRACK LISTING
3.49 Where's My Thing? 		DESCRIPTION
This is a promo cd single, and out of all the ones released in the US is the most limited (to 500 copies), so is the hardest to find. It has no front insert; the disc cd has an image of a skull printed on it, and the back has a picture of the "window with bones" flying through it.
