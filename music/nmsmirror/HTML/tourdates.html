Last Update: March 17, 1999 (version 5.0s)

Compiled by Jimmy Lang and Meg Jahnke, with help from Ken Fermaglich, David Arnold, Dan Delany, Neil Elliott, Chris Michael, Chris Kay, Skip Daly, Bruce Holtgren, Brad Parmerter & all the members of The National Midnight Star e-mail mailing list, our friends at The Spirit Of Rush, and readers of the newsgroup alt.music.rush

If you have any corrections or additions, e-mail them to: web@yyz.com To join the National Midnight Star Rush mailing list, send email to rush-request@tnms.com

In many cases where dates were questionable, I have included (?) to state so. Some dates gotten from various sources claim more than one location played on such a date. I have included (?) to mark these as well.

We are always looking for ticket stubs, interviews, articles or ads that verify any dates, especially those in the early tours. If you have anything and would like to scan it and e-mail it encoded (mime or uuencode), please send it to the above address.
