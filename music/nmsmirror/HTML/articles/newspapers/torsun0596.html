The Toronto Sun
May 27, 1996

Rush from the studio and on to the road
by JOHN SAKAMOTO

After an absence of almost three years, Rush is getting set to return to the world stage with a brand new studio release, Test For Echo.

According to the outstanding fan site run by the National Midnight Star, the album will feature 12 songs, 11 vocals, and one instrumental. It is scheduled for release in Canada and the U.S. on Sept. 3.

Despite earlier speculation that the band would not tour, Rush will indeed be hitting the road shortly after Test For Echo's release, a source close to the band confirmed Tuesday.

It will mark the first burst of activity from the trio since the October 1993 release of Counterparts. Geddy Lee, Neil Peart, and Alex Lifeson haven't performed together on stage since a tour-ending show at Toronto's Maple Leaf Gardens, two years ago this month.
