Directions

    The Bradley Center is located in downtown Mlwaukee on 4th between State and Highland. I-94 and I-43 run very close to the Bradley Center. From I-43, take Broadway to the 4th St. ramp. The Bradley Center and parking will be to the right on 4th. Or, take the Civic Center/Kilbourn exit from I-94. Make a left turn at the first stoplight and continue two blocks to the parking lot at 6th and State.

    Parking: Parking structures are located at 6th and State or at 4th and Highland. Handicapped parking is also available. Oversized vans should contact the Bradley Center at (414) 227-0797.
