Prime Mover
Vertigo RUSCD 14 (UK)
March 1988


TRACK LISTING
5.19 Prime Mover
4.14 Tai Shan
4.17 Distant Early Warning (live)
4.02 New World Man (live) 		DESCRIPTION
This is a limited-edition cd single in a cardboard sleeve, with the juggler on the cover. Each copy is numbered on the front; a total of 5,000 were pressed. The two live tracks are both from the "Grace Under Pressue" video soundtrack, which was never officially released on cd.
