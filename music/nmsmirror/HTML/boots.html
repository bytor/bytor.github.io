This page is still under construction, as well as those of the cd bootlegs. I am still slowly adding them to this page! I have listed them by the concert that represents the most on the cds; in some cases, more than one concert is on the cd.

Please note that all opinions on the quality of these cds are mine, and yours may not agree with what I say. There is a section for your reviews on the pages, so if you have one of them and want to offer your own opinion please do so.
